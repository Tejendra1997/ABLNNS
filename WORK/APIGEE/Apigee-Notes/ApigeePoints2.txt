APIGEE
======
• APIGEE ni setup chesukodaniki 3 ways unai
	1. subscription
	2. pay-as-you-go
	3. eval
• Subscription, Pay-as-you-go & eval lo ekuva cost vachi subscription endukante deeniki already intha ani pay chesestaam subscription roopam lo even though ah anni resources ni utilize chesukuna chesukokapoina anduke subscription anedi ekuva. whereas pay-as-you-go anedi entha vaadutamo anthe kabatti deeniki katchitamga intha ani chepalem so pay-as-you-go & subscription rendintilo compare cheste subscription anedi ekuva, kavaalante common sense tho alochinchu.
