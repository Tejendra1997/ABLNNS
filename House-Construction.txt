House Construction Process
===========================
• Mundu house plan kavaali papaer meeda
• tarvata plot clean chesi sidhanti cheta muggu geeyinchaali
• tarvat sankustaapana/ boomi pooja chestam
• Pillar gunthalu teeyistaam deene FOOTING antaaru
• pillars vesi vaatiki box la chutti vertical COLUMN CONCREATE vestaru & daani minimum 7 days CURING cheyali (water tho thadapadam & ee process morning and evening cheyali, edo tadipaam ante tadipaam ani kaakunda vaati meeda gona sanchulu veyali so that avi epudu wet gane untundi) so that basement strong ga untundi. Tarvaata ah gunthalani poodachali ala manaku nela mattam vastadi.
• Ipudu PLINTH BEEM lu vestaru ante dabba rekulu petti concreate vestaru (horizontal COLUMN CONCREATE), deeni yoka width anedi 1feet ye untundi. Deeni kuda minimum 7 days CURING cheyinchaali. (plinth beems chaala imp endukante daani meeda house vestaru kaabatti)
• Travaata pillars ni COLUMN CONCREATE vesi basement mark daaka kadataaru. (Ee basement mark anedi imp endukante deeni batte road nunchi house anedi entha helight lo undaali anedi ipude decide chesukuntaam kaabatti)
• Ipudu basement mark daaka ituka kattubadi chotooru & madyalo vestaru. Ee chottoru goda ni CURING cheyali minimum 7 days or atleast 4 days. Ee time lo ne septic tank ni basthrrom vache pakane guntha tavvi kadutaam.
• Basement chotturu goda ipoina tarvaata lopala erra matti gani tella matti gani veyali. (Erra matti anedu rate takuva but danilo konni problems kuda unaayanta) Ee matti ne mundu sagam varaku fill chesi neelu nimpali so that neelu matti lopaliki velli strong avudi, dani tarvaata migilina half kuda mati tho nimpu neelu posi gunapam tho atu itu podavali so that neelu kinda daaaka veli strong avtaadi. 2 days tarvaata gipsom tho matti ni thataali so that ekadaina loose unte tight avudi. Tarvata ah matti paina concreate veyali& parallel ga pillars ni CONCREATE COLUMNS  tho piki lepukkuntu povali. So ikaditho basement finish avudi.
