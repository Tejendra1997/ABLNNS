Interst Types in real world:
============================
• Maamulga bayata una vaddi vaalu rupai vaddi, rendu rupailu vaddi antuntaaru, Ade bank vaalu ithe 6%, 8%... ila antuntaaru. Nv edaina mundu percentages lo ke techuko endukante manaku percentage formulas ye telusu kaabatti.
• Munduga percentages ni rupai loki ela marchaalo chudaaam. First bank chepe percentages anevi edaina sare annual ga untaadi, year ki 12 months kaabatti ah percentage ni rupailoki marchaalante ila cheyali.
	For Eg: 8% anuko
			8/12 = 0.66 ante 66 paisalu
			Ade 10% anuko
			10/12 = 0.83 ante 83 paisalu
			Ade 12% anuko
			12/12 = 1 ante 1 rupee
• Ade rupailani, paisalni ni percentages ga convert cheyalante
	For Eg: rendu rupaila vaddi ante
			2*12 = 24 ante year ki 24% interest anamaata
			Ade rupai unaara ithe
			1.50*12 = 18 ante year ki 18% anamaata
			Ade 50paisalu ithe
			0.50*12 = 6 ante year ki 6% anamaata
• Konni basic convertions anevi epudu gurthu petukovali adi entante, 
	6% ante 50paissa ani
	12% ante 1rupee ani
	18% ante 1.50 rupees ani
	1rupee ante 12% ani
