architecture:
• Inthakumundu chepukunatlu istio nendi service mesh patern ni baaga implement chesina top most used tool. Ah service mesh lo chepina proxies ni ikada Envoyproxies antaaru alaane ah control plane component ni ikada istiod antaaru. Same apigee lo loaaga management plane (control plsnae) & runtime plane (data plane) untaayo ikada kuda ah istiod anedi control plane & ee gropu of envoy proxies anevi data plane.

configurations:
• Istio ni setup cheyadaaniki & dhani yoka configurations ki inthakumundu chepukuntalu microservice application tho paatuga kaakunda seperate ga untaadi and inko great thing entante ee configurations anevi kuberneties yaml configurations lone petachu ela ante ee istio CRD's ane concept use chestaadi and ee concept anedi kuberneties lo oka custom component lekka ante deeni use chesi kuberneties istiom, promethises... lanti third party applications ni connect cheyachu ala ipudu keberneties yaml file lone istio configurations CRD'S (like trafiic splitting config, retry config, traffic routing...) vaadi istio ni config cheyachu (Ee deeni valla malli ah specific istio technology nerchukovaalsina avasaram ledu, kubereneites tho ipodi). Ee config chestuna CRD's lo 2 most imp CRD'S or configurations entate VirtualService & Destination Rule anevi, virtualservice ante how to route traffic to a specific service anedi specify chesuntaadi and destination rule vachi specific service ki ruote inapudu ye policies / rules kavaali anevi define chestundi . ala configure chesinavi istio control plane ina istio d component lo ki veltaadi and inthakumudu chepukunatlu control plane ye anni microservice pods ki envoy proxies ni setup chestaadi andd vaatilo ee CRD's kuda velipothai inka ah envoy proxies ki ela behave cheyali ani telispotundi.
